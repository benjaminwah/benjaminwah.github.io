IEEE Awards and Recognitions Received

  • IEEE Fellow: “for contributions to the field of parallel processing,” 1991

  • IEEE Third Millennium Medal: "for outstanding contributions in the computer area,” 2000

  • IEEE Computer Society Technical Achievement Award: “for outstanding pioneering contributions to search optimization with parallel and neural network approaches,” 1998

  • IEEE  Computer Society Best Paper Awards: for his paper with T. Wang entitled "Constrained Simulated Annealing with
    Applications in Nonlinear Constrained Global Optimization" in the IEEE International Conference on Tools with Artificial Intelligence, 1999; for his paper entitled "An Efficient Heuristic for File Placement on Distributed Databases" in the IEEE International Conference on Software and Applications, 1981

  • IEEE Computer Society Outstanding Contribution Certificate: "for initiating and nurturing the IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering into a highly respected source of technical information,” 1992

  • IEEE Computer Society Meritorious Service Awards: “for strong leadership and unswerving dedication to the Society above and beyond the normal call of duty in the year 2000,” 2001; "for dedicated and significant services to the TAI conferences steering committee and as Program Chair of TAI’91," 1991

  • IEEE Computer Society Golden Core Member: "as one of the distinguished core of dedicated volunteers and staff whose leadership and service have made the IEEE Computer Society the world’s preeminent association of computing professionals," 1996

  • IEEE International Conference on Tools with Artificial Intelligence Technical Achievement Award: “for outstanding research contributions to IEEE ICTAI (1988-1997)”

  • IEEE Computer Society Certificates of Appreciation: “for exemplary and dedicated service as a member of the IEEE Computer Society Finance Committee,” 2001; “for outstanding leadership as a co-chair and member of the IEEE Computer Society Executive Director Search Committee,” 2001; "for services rendered as chairman, International Conference on Neural Networks," 1996; "for expert professional service with the highest standard while serving on the Computer Society’s 1994 IEEE Fellows Evaluation Committee,” 1994; "for thoughtful and conscientious evaluation of Fellows candidates during service on the 1993 Fellows Committee,” 1993

  • IEEE International Conference On Tools with Artificial Intelligence Outstanding Service Award: “for outstanding service as General Co-Chair of the IEEE– CS 10'th ICTAI,” 1998

  • IEEE Computer Society Distinguished Visitor, 1983-1986, 1989-1992
