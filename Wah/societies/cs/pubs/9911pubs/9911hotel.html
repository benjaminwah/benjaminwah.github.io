HOTEL MEAL ARRANGEMENTS

Please plan your agendas with a lunch break between 12 noon and 1:00 p.m. for a central buffet-style function that will be scheduled each day (Tuesday through Thursday) for all meetings week attendees. The only information you need to provide is a confirmed headcount for your group when you return the Meeting Request Form. Continental breakfasts and beverage breaks can still be made on an individual meeting basis. Note any special dietary requirements (i.e., vegetarian, low salt, low cholesterol, no MSG), in the space provided on the Meeting Request Form.

AGENDA BOOK

If your name appears on the Agenda Book report writers list, please submit your activity updates or any other Agenda Book materials to the headquarters office via regular or electronic mail (l.harris@computer.org) close of business by FRIDAY 22 October 1999shipping requests . Please limit all activity reports to one page or less. Special and address changes should be sent to me prior to the 5 November 1999 mailing date.

MEETING SUPPORT

A Meeting Request Form is provided to simplify your preparation for the meeting. Please return the form by Friday 22 October 1999 even if you have a "regularly scheduled meeting" in order that all meal details and audiovisual equipment expectations may be considered. [NOTE: If your meeting requires the use of an In Focus projector, please note this on the Meeting Request Form. We will bring three projectors to the meetings but their use will be on a first come, first serve basis. Digital projectors are prohibitively expensive to rent on-site.]

We recommend a conservative head count estimate for your functions as the hotel will include a 5% overage on beverage breaks and buffet type setups. Photocopying at the meeting site is always expensive, so we ask that you copy all materials and bring them with you. If no copying facilities are available to you, please send your materials to the society headquarters office no later than 5 November 1999. Our staff will be happy to copy and bring them to the meeting.
